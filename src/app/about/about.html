About KSi

Kingdom Support Initiatives is a God-ordained commission established to raising God’s children to change the world system and impact the world with the new life that is in Christ Jesus for a generation of new breed of kingdom citizens. Through her programmes, KSi will create platform for disciples to connect, interact, synergize, and strengthen one another to grow in God.

About ‘In His Footstep’

‘In His Footstep’ is a daily devotional to build continuous relationship with God. Through the word of God and the power of the Holy Spirit, the devotional seeks life to be transformed in Jesus Christ. The devotional is written by Kingdom Support Initiatives.

PUSH Network – Pray Until Something Happen Network

The goal of this network is to create a safe for people to prayer and intercession.

Inspire Impact

It is a podcast we are developing already to encourage and inspire God’s children to be bold for pursue whatever God is laying upon their hearts. Also, the podcast will create an opportunity for as many who want to explore Christianity.
